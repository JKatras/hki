Steps - How it Works

1. Plan

Meal Planning has never been easier with this amazing meal plan calendar. Drag–n–drop breakfast, lunch, dinner, and even school snacks into the calendar and then post for the family to see. Who knew meal planning could be so fun?

2. Shop

Each meal plan comes with a customized shopping list including everything you need to quickly tackle that dreaded trip to the grocery store. We’ve created our Meal Planner to handle everything but cooking the meal itself, helping parents, just like you, achieve the dream of seamlessly putting a wholesome dinner on the table – every night.

FAQs

Where can I find the meal planner?

The meal planner is available to members only. Upon payment and registration, members will establish a Username and Passcode to gain access to the site. From the Members Home page, users can choose from 52 weeks of pre-determined meal plans or use the HKI meal plan calendar to mix-n-match recipes to best suit their hectic week. Click here to see just one of over 250 dinner recipes on our site.

What is Healthy Kids Inc.?

Consider it a meal planner like no other. Created by two busy parents desperate for help, HKI shows parents what to cook, how to cook it, and how to get your kids to eat it — while still managing that hectic schedule.

How do I know that the meals are healthy?

Each meal has been analyzed by the Healthy Kids Inc., Registered Dietitian. The entire meal has been analyzed, not just a portion (meat or side dish). Fat, calories, sodium, and sugar were all taken into account when determining if the recipe is healthy and should be included on the HKI site. Members will also be able to view the nutritional analysis for each dish.

Can I print the recipes and shopping list?

Yes. Each recipe, meal plan, and shopping list can be printed.

Can the recipes be shared with non-members?

No. The Healthy Kids Inc., website is a members-only site. Recipes within the site cannot be shared with non members or posted to social sharing sites.

How do I pay for my membership?

Simply click here to be taken to our checkout page. The HKI cart accepts all major credit cards.

How do I cancel my membership?

Members that wish to cancel their membership should email the HKI support team at info@healthykidsinc.com. We offer a full 30-day money back guarantee. If you’re not satisfied, simply let us know and your payment will be refunded within 5-7 days of request.

Do you offer meal plans that cater to special diets (Paleo, Gluten Free, Vegetarian, Plant Based, Nut Free, etc.)?

The HKI meal plan does allow the ability to search Gluten Free, Vegetarian, and Nut Free recipes. Many traditional recipes can also easily modified to eliminate allergens.

What if I don’t like the meal plan suggested for the entire week?

That’s the beauty of the HKI site — members can easily mix and match recipes to create a week that works best for their families. Custom weeks can then be saved for future reference.

My kid won’t eat the meals in the planner. What should I do?

Getting kids involved with healthy eating can take time. Don’t give up! It may take 10 tries, and various cooking applications, before a child warms up to the taste of a particular vegetable. The HKI meal planner includes a variety of different cooking applications for vegetables (roasting, sautéing, baking, steaming, etc.). Healthy Kids Inc., also offers a number of resources to help your kids (and even adults) overcome picky eating tendencies. You can download our FREE Picky Eater eBook here.

Can I reach out to the Owners, Chef, or Dietitian at any time?

Founder, Mandy Curry, welcomes the opportunity to talk one-on-one with members about any challenges they may be having. She can be reached at mandy@healthykidsinc.com.

Do you specialize in weight loss?

The HKI site focuses on a healthy eating lifestyle. While our meals make use of healthy ingredients, we can not guarantee weight loss. If a member is looking for specialized weight loss counseling, this can be provided by the HKI Dietitian at an additional cost. Inquiries on weight loss counseling should be sent to mandy@healthykidsinc.com.
